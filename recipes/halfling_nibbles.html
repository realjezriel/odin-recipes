Halfling Oatmeal Sweet Nibbles

Delicious Halfling Oatmeal Sweet Nibbles, freshly baked!

Dear reader, hope you enjoy this short demo. Please read on to see if the cookies contain REAL Halflings within them!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups (180 g) rolled oats
  • 1 tsp. (1.25 g) baking soda
  • ½ tsp. (3 g) kosher salt
  • 1 ½ cups (255 g) packed light brown sugar
  • 1 cup (128 g) unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 tsp. (10 ml) pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (150 g) semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup (150 g) butterscotch chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and line 2 large baking trays with parchment paper
  2. Whisk flour, roll oats, baking soda, kosher salt into a bowl
  3. Beat the butter and brown sugar in a bowl until well combined
  4. Add egg and vanila extract and whisk till smooth
  5. Stir in dry ingredients, add chocolate and butterscotch chips
  6. Bake the cookies for 15 minutes, rotating the positions half way through
  7. After the cookies have been baked, allow the cookies to cool on a tray for 3 minutes
